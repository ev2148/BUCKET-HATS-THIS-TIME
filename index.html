About us

Welcome to Bucket Hats™, your one stop shop for all types of bucket hats! We're dedicated towards giving you only the VERY best, with a focus on unique styles, durability, and affordable prices! Founded in 2021, Bucket Hats™ has come a long way from its humble beginnings. In Eva’s first month living in Abu Dhabi, she was eager to find a way to survive the 107 degree temperatures. Luckily, her university gave her a bucket hat as a welcoming gift, and her love and passion for bucket hats has grown ever since. Now, she dedicates her time and energy towards sharing the beauty of bucket hats, and her efforts have resulted in a booming online store. Bucket Hats™ now serves customers from all over the world, and we are beyond excited to continue the journey, one hat at a time.
